What it does

A small Copy button on still images in the feed, search, and boards.

Copies a clean PNG straight to your system clipboard.

Carousel pins copy the slide that's actually visible; video pins are skipped.

Optional higher-res fetch when the page exposes a larger URL, off by default.

Strips Pinterest hover UI before capture, so you never paste junk.

Never reads your clipboard, never talks to a backend.

Privacy

Pluck runs entirely on your device. It does not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Pinterest account info, pin/image URLs, or copied images
  • Clipboard contents, browsing history, or search queries
  • Device identifiers, analytics, telemetry, or crash reports

The only settings stored are local booleans (enabled, diagnostics, higher-quality). The one possible network request, an optional i.pinimg.com image fetch, happens only when you click Copy with higher-quality mode on, without credentials or referrer, and is never retained.
